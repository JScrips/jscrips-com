About me:

My name is Terrance Brown. I'm a 28 year old, brand new web-developer, born and raised in South Central, Los Angeles. I graduated from California State University, Northridge with a communications degree, and went to high school at Crenshaw High. I've been an avid gamer since the age of 7, and I've played almost every MMORPG there is on the internet (probably). Aside from gaming, I also really enjoy hanging with my friends, working out at the gym, and staying at home, doing absolutely nothing. Over the span of my life, I've held almost 30 jobs, no exaggeration. I've worked everywhere and have done everything; from bowling alleys, to kitches, to offices, to theme parks, to retail stores, to banks. I've held a LOT of different jobs, in search of stability, in search of something I enjoyed, in search of answers. 90% of the time just searching to try something new.
